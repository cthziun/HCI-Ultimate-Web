Manage Your Ultimate Team Seamlessly

UltiMate is a design-driven HCI+D project aimed at simplifying communication and coordination in ultimate frisbee teams. Built with empathy, iteration, and testing at its core.

Dual Display Functionality

UltiMate offers a unique dual display experience, tailored for both iPhones and iPads.

iPhone: Player Evaluation

Evaluate player performance on the go with your iPhone. Access player stats, provide feedback, and track progress in real-time.

iPad: Team Management & Game Recording

Set up your team, manage players, and record games live with your iPad. Enjoy a larger screen for strategic planning and detailed game analysis.
